What Is Going On There

Posted on 2012-06-06
Words count in article: 270 | Reading time ≈ 2

It has been years since I last opened this page.
How time flies!
Everything goes on as usual, this is precisely my tranquil life:
When do I get up everyday?
Well it depends, my clock rings so many times every morning that even myself can’t figure it out.
But no earlier than what I did one year ago.
Years may wrinkle your skin,
but never can them wrinkle your solid dream if you possess one,
whatever it is,
a higher grade, a higher position, more fame and forture,
just what you have in mind at the moment,
that is it, as long as you have one,
embrace it, protect it and yell it out every morning you open your eyes,
that is my persperation, that is what I am yearning for.

Sometimes I am down or weary, though
I do not wanna talk with anyone at times
we just keep a distance,
far enough that you are not in my sight, beyond my reach,
near enough that I can peep you when I have attempt,
and just it, no more, no less,
this is where I am going.

Sometimes I wanna find a place where there is nobody who knows me.
And start a new phase,
relish the morning sunshine, soft breath, a clear blue sky in June,
jogging down the old track which I have been running through for many times,
the night is so silent,
I hope so,
I imagine,
I wish I am immersed in serenity,
stary night,
spreading like my mind,
blue water flows calmly,
towards the infinite horizon where the sky and sea meets.

写在宏福的第一年
告别的时刻
